# Atlassian

## Summary

Atlassian is an enterprise software company headquartered in Sydney that develops widely-adopted tools for software development and collaboration, including Jira, Confluence, Bitbucket, and others. Atlassian Intelligence is their AI assistant.

## Key Facts

- **Core Products**: Jira (issue tracking/project management), Confluence (wiki/documentation), Bitbucket (Git hosting)
- **Market Share**: ~40% of PM software market by 2026
- **Jira Features**: 3000+ integrations, deep customization (workflows, fields, automations), ML-backed search (JQL), Atlassian Intelligence for content generation and summaries
- **Compliance**: SOC2, ISO 27001, GDPR compliance
- **Integrations**: Slack, Microsoft Teams, GitHub, GitLab, Bitbucket, Jenkins
- **AI Assistant**: Atlassian Intelligence provides in-editor content generation, tone adjustments, issue summaries, work breakdown suggestions
- **Cloud Architecture**: Runs on Kubernetes (Micros custom PaaS), using Kafka and Redis for feeds
- **Pricing**: Jira Premium ~$17/user/month (2025)

## Implications

- **Market Leadership**: Atlassian's dominance makes it the primary competitive benchmark
- **Enterprise Features**: Strong compliance and security focus appeals to regulated industries
- **AI Strategy**: Atlassian's early adoption of AI features signals the competitive importance of intelligent assistance
- **Integration Hub**: 3000+ apps create powerful ecosystem; new entrants must support key integrations to compete
