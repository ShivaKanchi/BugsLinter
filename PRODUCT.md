# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Engineering leads, delivery managers, and software teams who manage bug fixes, release readiness, and issue triage. They use Bugslinter while working through bug context, coordinating work across teammates, and connecting issue detail to delivery decisions without adding process overhead.

## Product Purpose

Bugslinter helps delivery teams ship bugs to resolution with AI-assisted issue context while preserving human judgment. It exists to centralize bug details, keep release and workflow state aligned, connect issue context to coding-agent flows, and reduce the friction of bloated issue trackers.

## Brand Personality

Pragmatic, composed, and trustworthy. The product feels clear and capable, with a human-first delivery voice rather than hype or chaos.

## Anti-references

Not a bloated Jira-style tracker, not a flashy AI "agent toy," and not a noisy enterprise dashboard. Avoid dense admin panels, gradient hero-metric clichés, and styling that feels like crypto/neon or generic SaaS marketing.

## Design Principles

- Make delivery context visible quickly and keep the workflow easy to scan.
- Show AI assistance as structured support, not a replacement for human control.
- Keep the experience calm and focused; use motion sparingly and only to clarify state.
- Favor readable hierarchy, strong legibility, and deliberate spacing.
- Build for team trust with clear affordances, consistent feedback, and inclusive interactions.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA contrast, strong focus states, keyboard-first navigation, semantic structure, and `prefers-reduced-motion` support. Designed for users who need reliable clarity in a delivery workflow.
